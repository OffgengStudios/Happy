Consent to Participate

Purpose

The HAPPY Programme collects participant data to support training, placement, reporting, and programme improvement. Your data helps us connect Ghana's youth with employment opportunities.

Data Collected

  • Name, age, sex, contact details, education level, and location
  • Employment status, sector interest, and CV / skills information
  • Disability status, refugee/displaced status, and training details where applicable

Data Sharing

Data may be shared with Jobberman Ghana, Agri-Impact Limited, funding partners (including Mastercard Foundation), and approved data processors for monitoring, evaluation, and programme delivery.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, restriction, or withdrawal of consent at any time by contacting your HAPPY field officer. Privacy Policy: mastercardfdn.org/privacy
